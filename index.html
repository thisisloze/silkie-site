Silkie Secrets

Black Skin Under the fluff, their skin, bones, and meat are dark blue/black (Fibromelanosis).
5 Toes Most chickens have 4 toes. Silkies have 5 or 6! Extra toes = extra cute.
Can't Fly Their feathers lack hooks, so they feel like fur and can't catch air. Low fences work great.
Broody Queens Silkies are famous mothers. They will happily hatch eggs from other breeds or ducks.

Worm Wisdom

Surface Dwellers Red Wigglers live in the top 6 inches of soil and love leaf litter, unlike deep-digging earthworms.
Voracious Eaters A healthy colony can eat half its own body weight in scraps every single day.
Black Gold Worm castings are 5-11x richer in nutrients than regular soil. Superfood for plants.
